About Us

Compass Insurance Adjusters of Canada Inc. is a boutique Independent Claims Adjusting firm located in Winnipeg, Manitoba. We act on behalf of insurers, government, cargo insurers, and large self-insured entities.

Our Services

We have a particular focus on investigating and resolving claims related to:

  • Property damage to foreclosed properties including recoveries from HO insurers
  • Commercial property losses of all types
  • Liability claims
  • Losses involving Certificates of Insurance
  • Losses with government and government agencies

We also:

  • Provide regional reach for out-of-province IA firms
  • Conduct Pre-load, loaded, and damaged cargo surveys
  • Have a sub-office in Pinawa, Manitoba to service Eastern Manitoba
